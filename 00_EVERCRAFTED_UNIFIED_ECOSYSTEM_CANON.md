# 00_EVERCRAFTED_UNIFIED_ECOSYSTEM_CANON

## Purpose

Evercrafted is an emotion-aware procedural floral design operating system.

It is not simply an AI wreath generator, a prompt tool, or a collection of unrelated creative apps. Evercrafted is a layered ecosystem that converts human memory, emotional intent, seasonal context, inventory constraints, and design rules into deterministic floral geometry, blueprint data, visual previews, manufacturing instructions, and commerce-ready outputs.

The master canon of Evercrafted is:

> AI interprets. Geometry places.

AI is used for emotional interpretation, narrative understanding, symbolic matching, content generation, and design explanation. Geometry is responsible for actual placement, structure, distribution, clustering, balance, and manufacturability.

---

## The Three-Layer Ecosystem

### 1. Evercrafted OS

Evercrafted OS is the internal engine and operational intelligence layer.

It contains the Emotion Engine, Formula Engine, Wreath DNA Engine, Placement Engine, Placement Visualization Engine, Blueprint Compiler, BuildBloom Manufacturing Engine, Inventory Engine, Blueprint Evaluator, System Console, API infrastructure, and schema infrastructure.

The uploaded System Console HTML belongs here. It is not just prototype UI. It is the visible operating console for the engine layer.

### 2. Evercrafted Studio

Evercrafted Studio is the creator-facing SaaS product.

It turns the OS into usable workflows for wreath designers, Etsy sellers, floral artists, decorators, and creative businesses.

Studio includes Memory Weaver, Design Studio, Inventory Weaver, Render Studio, Design Library, Build Sheet generation, Marketplace publishing, and Blueprint exports.

### 3. Moodoor

Moodoor is the consumer-facing emotional commerce layer.

Consumers do not see the engine. They answer simple mood and home-context questions, and Moodoor uses Evercrafted OS to match them to wreaths, visual concepts, or purchasable products.

---

## Canonical Engine Spine

Every major Evercrafted design flow follows this sequence:

1. Emotion input
2. Emotion Engine interpretation
3. Formula Engine selection
4. Wreath DNA trait generation
5. Placement Engine geometry
6. Placement Visualization Engine rendering
7. Blueprint Compiler normalization
8. BuildBloom manufacturing translation
9. Render Studio / Marketplace / Moodoor outputs

This is the core system spine.

---

## Non-Negotiable Design Doctrine

Evercrafted must preserve asymmetry over generic symmetry, movement over static layout, premium faux floral realism, negative space as a designed feature, believable stem direction, manufacturable placement, emotional coherence, deterministic geometry, and reusable blueprint data.

Evercrafted must avoid random AI placement, centered floral blobs, bouquet-style wreaths, fresh florist bouquet aesthetics, low-end craft-store styling, non-buildable compositions, and disconnected app logic.

---

## What Makes Evercrafted Defensible

The moat is not the image prompt.

The moat is the system:

Emotion → Formula → DNA → Placement → Visualization → Blueprint → Manufacturing → Commerce

Evercrafted’s value is the ability to turn subjective emotion into repeatable, structured, manufacturable design intelligence.
