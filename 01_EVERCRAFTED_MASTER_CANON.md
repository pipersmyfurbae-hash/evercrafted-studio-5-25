# 01_EVERCRAFTED_MASTER_CANON

## Brand Definition

Evercrafted is a premium emotional design intelligence platform for faux floral wreath creation and creative commerce.

It helps creators transform memories, moods, seasonal stories, customer intent, and inventory into professional wreath designs that are beautiful, buildable, sellable, and emotionally specific.

## Positioning

Preferred positioning:

> The emotional operating system for floral design.

Alternative positioning:
- Emotion-aware procedural design for modern wreath creators.
- Memory-to-blueprint intelligence for premium faux floral design.
- Creative infrastructure for wreath makers, sellers, and decorative designers.

Avoid positioning Evercrafted as a generic AI wreath generator, simple prompt generator, craft hobby tool, Canva clone, or random image generator.

## Core Promise

Evercrafted turns emotional intent into structured design output.

The platform gives creators design direction, placement logic, material guidance, visual preview, manufacturing clarity, listing support, and commerce support.

## Brand Personality

Evercrafted should feel premium, emotionally intelligent, precise, editorial, calm, structured, artistic, and technically grounded.

It should not feel gimmicky, childish, chaotic, generic, overly crafty, or cheaply seasonal.

## Brand Standards

Every output should preserve visual restraint, premium material language, emotional clarity, practical buildability, structured formatting, and designer-level composition logic.

## Canon Rule

> AI interprets. Geometry places.
