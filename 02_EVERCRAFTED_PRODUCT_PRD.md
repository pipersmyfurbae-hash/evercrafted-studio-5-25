# 02_EVERCRAFTED_PRODUCT_PRD

## Product Overview

Evercrafted is a modular SaaS ecosystem for faux floral wreath designers, Etsy sellers, decor creators, and creative businesses. It is powered by Evercrafted OS, the internal engine layer that translates emotional input into procedural blueprint systems.

The commercial ecosystem contains two primary product surfaces:

1. Evercrafted Studio — creator SaaS platform
2. Moodoor — consumer emotional commerce platform

## Primary Users

Creator users include wreath makers, Etsy sellers, floral artists, handmade decor businesses, interior stylists, and seasonal product creators.

Consumer users include homeowners, gift buyers, seasonal decorators, and customers who choose products by mood rather than technical design language.

## Subscription Concept

### Free
Limited monthly designs, saved examples, marketplace browsing.

### Maker
Core creator workflows: Memory Weaver, Design Studio, Inventory Weaver, Render Studio, Design Library.

### Studio Pro
All tools, exports, build sheets, marketplace publishing, client-ready outputs, advanced intelligence.

## Commercial Value

Evercrafted helps creators design faster, reduce material waste, create more consistent products, write stronger listings, generate visual prompts, turn inventory into sellable collections, build emotional product stories, and scale a design business.

## Product Strategy

The MVP should prove the core engine, not every possible app.

The first marketable product should be:

> A creator platform that turns memory, mood, and inventory into a buildable faux wreath blueprint with visual preview, render prompt, and build sheet.
