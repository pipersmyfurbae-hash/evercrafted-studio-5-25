# 19_MOODOOR_PRD

## Purpose

Moodoor is the consumer emotional commerce layer powered by Evercrafted OS.

## Consumer Flow

1. How do you want your home to feel?
2. What season or occasion is this for?
3. What does your door/home look like?
4. Moodoor returns a matching wreath.

## Relationship to Evercrafted

Moodoor uses Evercrafted OS invisibly. Consumers should not see technical design language.

## MVP Status

Moodoor is not part of Evercrafted MVP V1. It should remain a V2/V3 commerce expansion.
