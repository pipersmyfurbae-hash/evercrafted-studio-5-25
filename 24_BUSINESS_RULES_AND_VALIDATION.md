# 24_BUSINESS_RULES_AND_VALIDATION

## Purpose

This file defines hard system rules.

## Design Rules

- preserve asymmetry
- avoid centered floral blobs
- preserve negative space
- maintain premium faux floral realism
- no random AI placement
- all placements must be deterministic

## Material Rules

MVP should support faux materials only. Fresh flower logic is out of scope.

## Blueprint Validation

Blueprint must include emotion, formula, DNA, placements, visualization settings, and manufacturing object.

## Manufacturing Rules

Build sheet must be physically plausible: materials exist, stem counts are realistic, attachment sequence makes sense, and design has visible structure.
