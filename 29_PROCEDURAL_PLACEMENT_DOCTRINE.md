# 29_PROCEDURAL_PLACEMENT_DOCTRINE

## Core Doctrine

> AI interprets. Geometry places.

This is the foundational rule of Evercrafted.

## Why This Matters

AI is excellent at interpreting memory, emotion, language, style, and symbolic meaning.

AI is not reliable as the sole source of spatial placement, manufacturable geometry, repeatable composition, or structural balance.

Therefore Evercrafted separates interpretation from placement.

## AI Responsibilities

AI may classify emotion, suggest formula, describe design intent, explain choices, write listings, generate render prompts, and summarize blueprint logic.

## Geometry Responsibilities

Geometry must place elements, control density, enforce spacing, preserve negative space, calculate radial positions, manage clusters, assign anchor zones, and prevent symmetry problems.

## Controlled Variation

The system may use seeded variation. Variation must be repeatable, bounded, explainable, and stored in blueprint metadata.

## Forbidden Behavior

Do not allow unbounded random placement, AI-only layout generation, image-first design logic, visually pretty but non-buildable output, or layout that cannot be reconstructed from data.

## Final Principle

Evercrafted designs must be emotionally expressive and mathematically reconstructable.
