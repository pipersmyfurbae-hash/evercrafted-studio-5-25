# 37_CANONICAL_TERMINOLOGY

## Purpose

This document standardizes Evercrafted naming.

Use these names consistently in code, UI, docs, and developer handoff.

---

## Ecosystem Terms

### Evercrafted

The full company/product ecosystem.

### Evercrafted OS

The internal engine and operational intelligence layer.

### Evercrafted Studio

The creator-facing SaaS application.

### Moodoor

The consumer-facing emotional commerce platform.

---

## Engine Terms

### Emotion Engine

Interprets memory, mood, and emotional language.

### Formula Engine

Selects composition archetype.

### Wreath DNA Engine

Generates procedural design traits.

### Placement Engine

Calculates deterministic geometry.

### Placement Visualization Engine

Renders placement data visually.

### Blueprint Compiler

Compiles engine outputs into EC_CANON_V1.

### BuildBloom

Manufacturing intelligence system.

### Inventory Engine

Material availability, roles, cost, and substitution logic.

---

## Data Terms

### EC_CANON_V1

The canonical blueprint schema.

### Blueprint

A complete structured wreath design object.

### Placement

A single positioned material instance.

### Zone

An angular region of the wreath.

### Anchor

A key placement point used to organize a cluster.

### Cluster

A group of related placements.

### Formula

A structural composition archetype.

### DNA

Procedural design trait profile.

---

## UI Terms

### Design Studio

Main creator editor.

### Memory Weaver

Emotional intake experience.

### Inventory Weaver

Inventory management experience.

### Render Studio

Prompt and visual-output generator.

### Design Library

Saved blueprint archive.

### Build Sheet

Manufacturing output from BuildBloom.

---

## Forbidden / Discouraged Terms

Avoid:
- random AI wreath generator
- automatic flower placer
- craft template maker
- AI art wreath
- prompt-only tool
- generic design generator

Preferred:
- emotional design engine
- procedural blueprint
- deterministic placement
- manufacturable design
- blueprint intelligence
- placement visualization
- creative operating system

---

## Naming Rule

Use “Engine” for backend/intelligence systems.

Use “Studio” or “Weaver” for creator-facing product experiences.

Use “Canon” for official doctrine/schema files.
