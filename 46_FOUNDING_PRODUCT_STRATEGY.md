# 46_FOUNDING_PRODUCT_STRATEGY

## Purpose

This document defines the initial launch strategy for Evercrafted Studio MVP.

---

## Initial Market

Primary market:
- Etsy wreath sellers
- premium faux floral creators
- seasonal decor businesses
- creative entrepreneurs

Avoid targeting:
- general AI users
- enterprise teams
- hobby crafters
- mass-market consumers

---

## MVP Core Promise

“Turn memory, mood, and inventory into a manufacturable premium wreath blueprint.”

That is the core launch promise.

---

## Primary MVP Workflow

1. Enter emotional concept.
2. Generate structured blueprint.
3. Visualize layout.
4. Generate build sheet.
5. Generate render prompt.
6. Save reusable design.

---

## Initial Differentiator

The moat is:
- deterministic placement
- emotional intelligence
- manufacturable output
- premium faux floral realism

Not:
- generic AI images
- random prompts
- instant art generation

---

## Launch Strategy

Phase 1:
- founder-led demos
- waitlist
- creator onboarding
- limited beta access

Phase 2:
- paid Studio subscriptions
- creator showcases
- blueprint library growth

Phase 3:
- Marketplace
- Moodoor
- API/platform expansion
