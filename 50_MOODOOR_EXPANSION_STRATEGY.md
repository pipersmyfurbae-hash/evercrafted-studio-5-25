# 50_MOODOOR_EXPANSION_STRATEGY

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term Moodoor direction.

---

## What Moodoor Is

Moodoor is the emotional commerce layer powered by Evercrafted OS.

Consumers do not see:
- placement logic
- blueprint structure
- DNA systems
- deterministic geometry

Consumers experience:
- mood matching
- home aesthetics
- emotional recommendation
- seasonal storytelling

---

## Consumer Flow

1. “How do you want your home to feel?”
2. Mood analysis
3. Product recommendation
4. Purchase or customization

---

## Long-Term Vision

Moodoor may expand into:
- home decor
- seasonal environments
- table styling
- event styling
- emotional retail recommendations

---

## Important Rule

Moodoor is not the MVP.

Evercrafted Studio must succeed first.
