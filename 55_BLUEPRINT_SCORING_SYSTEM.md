# 55_BLUEPRINT_SCORING_SYSTEM

## Purpose

Defines the scoring model used by the Blueprint Evaluator Engine.

---

## Scoring Categories

### Composition Balance — 25%
Measures:
- visual balance
- negative space
- cluster harmony

### Movement — 20%
Measures:
- directional flow
- greenery extension
- silhouette rhythm

### Emotional Coherence — 20%
Measures:
- mood consistency
- density alignment
- formula appropriateness

### Manufacturability — 20%
Measures:
- realistic spacing
- practical construction
- believable material usage

### Originality — 15%
Measures:
- repetitive layouts
- overly generic clustering
- formula variation

---

## Score Thresholds

### 90–100
Exceptional Evercrafted-quality blueprint.

### 80–89
Strong blueprint with minor improvements possible.

### 70–79
Usable blueprint with visible weaknesses.

### Below 70
Needs major revision.

---

## Automatic Warnings

The system should flag:
- excessive symmetry
- overcrowded center
- low movement
- unrealistic density
- weak focal hierarchy
- excessive filler usage
