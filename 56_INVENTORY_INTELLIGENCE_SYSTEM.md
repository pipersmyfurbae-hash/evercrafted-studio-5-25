# 56_INVENTORY_INTELLIGENCE_SYSTEM

## Purpose

This document defines the future inventory intelligence layer.

---

## Core Goal

Make blueprint generation aware of:
- available inventory
- material compatibility
- substitution logic
- seasonal inventory trends
- profitability

---

## Future Capabilities

### Material Substitution

Example:
“Cream peony unavailable → substitute ivory ranunculus with adjusted density.”

### Inventory-Aware Formula Selection

Example:
- low greenery inventory → reduce extension-heavy formulas
- high filler inventory → increase airy compositions

### Profitability Analysis

Calculate:
- material cost
- labor estimate
- estimated margin

### Seasonal Intelligence

Track:
- most-used materials by season
- trending palettes
- inventory depletion trends

---

## Long-Term Potential

Inventory intelligence can become:
- predictive ordering
- wholesale recommendation
- collection planning
- automated seasonal prep
