# 58_MARKETPLACE_AND_BLUEPRINT_COMMERCE

## Purpose

Defines the future blueprint marketplace system.

---

## Marketplace Concept

Creators can:
- sell blueprint files
- sell build sheets
- sell collections
- license formulas
- distribute seasonal packs

---

## Marketplace Products

### Blueprint Pack
Contains:
- EC_CANON_V1 blueprint
- build sheet
- render prompts
- material list

### Seasonal Collection
Contains:
- multiple coordinated blueprints
- matching palette systems
- coordinated visual storytelling

---

## Commerce Rules

Marketplace must support:
- creator attribution
- licensing terms
- payout tracking
- blueprint ownership

---

## Long-Term Differentiator

Evercrafted is not selling images.

It is selling:
- structured design intelligence
- manufacturable composition systems
- reusable emotional blueprints
