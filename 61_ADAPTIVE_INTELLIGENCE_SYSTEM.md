# 61_ADAPTIVE_INTELLIGENCE_SYSTEM

## Purpose

Defines the future adaptive intelligence layer for Evercrafted.

---

## Core Goal

Allow Evercrafted to learn from:
- successful blueprints
- creator behavior
- marketplace performance
- emotional response patterns
- inventory trends

without abandoning deterministic placement principles.

---

## Important Rule

Adaptive intelligence may influence:
- recommendations
- formula weighting
- palette suggestions
- collection direction

Adaptive intelligence must not:
- replace deterministic geometry
- introduce uncontrolled randomness
- bypass placement validation

---

## Learning Sources

### Creator Actions
- blueprint saves
- exports
- duplications
- collection creation

### Marketplace Signals
- purchases
- favorites
- repeat downloads

### Evaluator Data
- high-scoring compositions
- successful asymmetry patterns
- emotional coherence correlations

---

## Future Capabilities

- adaptive formula ranking
- emotional trend prediction
- successful composition clustering
- creator style fingerprinting
- collection optimization

---

## Long-Term Vision

Evercrafted becomes:
- emotionally adaptive
- procedurally intelligent
- creator-aware
while preserving blueprint determinism.
