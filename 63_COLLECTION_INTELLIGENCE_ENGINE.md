# 63_COLLECTION_INTELLIGENCE_ENGINE

## Purpose

Defines the collection intelligence layer.

---

## Core Goal

Generate coordinated product collections instead of isolated designs.

---

## Collection Intelligence Responsibilities

- seasonal coordination
- palette continuity
- emotional progression
- silhouette variation
- pricing ladder support
- inventory-aware grouping

---

## Example Collection Types

### Seasonal Collection
Spring / Summer / Autumn / Winter

### Emotional Collection
Quiet Light / Coastal Calm / Romantic Gathering

### Interior Collection
Modern Farmhouse / Moody Cottage / Editorial Neutral

---

## Collection Rules

A collection should contain:
- visual cohesion
- formula diversity
- emotional continuity
- material consistency

---

- coordinated render prompts
