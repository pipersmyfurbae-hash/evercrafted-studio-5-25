# 66_FUTURE_CATEGORY_EXPANSION

## Purpose

Defines how Evercrafted expands beyond wreaths.

---

## Core Principle

Evercrafted is not fundamentally a wreath company.

It is a procedural emotional decorative design platform.

---

## Future Categories

### Garlands
- mantle systems
- staircase draping
- seasonal greenery flows

### Tablescapes
- centerpiece layouts
- emotional dining environments

### Floral Installations
- wedding installations
- retail floral structures
- event environments

### Seasonal Interiors
- doorway styling
- shelf styling
- coordinated home decor

### Visual Merchandising
- hospitality
- boutique retail
- branded installations

---

## Expansion Rule

Every future category should preserve:
- emotional intelligence
- deterministic placement
- blueprint systems
- manufacturable logic
