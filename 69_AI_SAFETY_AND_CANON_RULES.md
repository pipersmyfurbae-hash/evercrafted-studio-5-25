# 69_AI_SAFETY_AND_CANON_RULES

## Purpose

Defines the canon protection and AI safety doctrine.

---

## Core Principle

Evercrafted must preserve:
- design quality
- manufacturability
- emotional coherence
- procedural integrity

---

## Forbidden Behaviors

### Forbidden Placement Behavior
- random uncontrolled placement
- AI-only layout generation
- center-heavy clustering
- excessive symmetry
- unrealistic density

### Forbidden Rendering Behavior
- generic bouquet aesthetics
- fresh florist styling
- low-quality craft styling
- impossible floral scale

### Forbidden AI Outputs
- malformed blueprint JSON
- missing required metadata
- non-repeatable placements

---

## Validation Requirements

Every blueprint should validate:
- placement ranges
- role integrity
- formula compatibility
- emotional consistency
- manufacturability

---

## Canon Rule

> AI interprets. Geometry places.

This rule is non-negotiable.
